Earth - Our Home Planet

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to support life. It has a rich diversity of landforms, climates, and ecosystems. Approximately 71% of the Earth's surface is covered by water.

  • Size: 12,742 km in diameter
  • Water Content: 71% of Earth's surface is covered in water
  • Distance from the Sun: 149.6 million kilometers (1 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 365.25 days
  • Moons: 1 (The Moon)
