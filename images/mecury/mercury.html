Mercury - The Smallest Planet

Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in the Solar System. Despite its proximity to the Sun, it experiences extreme temperature fluctuations, as it has almost no atmosphere to trap heat.

  • Size: 4,880 km in diameter
  • Water Content: No known liquid water, but there may be ice in the shadowed craters at the poles.
  • Distance from the Sun: 57.9 million kilometers (0.39 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 88 Earth days
  • Moons: None
  • Atmosphere: Very thin atmosphere made mostly of oxygen, sodium, hydrogen, helium, and potassium.
