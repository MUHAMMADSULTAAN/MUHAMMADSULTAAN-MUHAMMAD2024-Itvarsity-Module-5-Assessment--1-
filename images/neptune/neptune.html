Neptune - The Blue Giant

Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in our Solar System. Known for its striking blue color, Neptune is a gas giant with intense winds and storm systems. It has 14 known moons, including Triton, which has geysers that erupt nitrogen gas.

  • Size: 49,244 km in diameter
  • Water Content: Neptune is believed to have a large amount of water, methane, and ammonia in its atmosphere.
  • Distance from the Sun: 4.5 billion kilometers (30.1 AU)
  • Orbital Period: 164.8 Earth years
  • Moons: 14 moons (including Triton, which orbits Neptune in the opposite direction)
  • Atmosphere: Mostly hydrogen, helium, and methane, giving the planet its blue appearance.
